# Acme Expansion Proposal (v3)

## Overview

Acme is expanding the platform across its EU subsidiaries. This proposal covers a
$1.2M ARR annual expansion targeted to close this week.

## Commercial Terms

Total contract value: $1.2M ARR, annual, net-30. Includes platform expansion,
onboarding, and standard support.

## Scope

Provision Acme's EU subsidiaries, migrate existing workspaces, and enable the
expansion modules requested during discovery.

## Data Residency

EU data residency is to be confirmed in the statement of work. Acme requires all EU
customer data to remain resident in EU data centers as a condition of the expansion.
